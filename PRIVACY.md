# Privacy Policy for EchoX Stream Helper

This extension is entirely open-source and respects user privacy.

## Data Collection & Usage
- **Zero Tracking:** The extension does not collect, store, log, or transmit any user data, personal information, or browsing history.
- **Local Execution:** All streaming extraction processes happen entirely locally inside your browser context footprint. 
- **No Third-Party Sharing:** No data is ever shared with third parties or external servers except for the direct video translation endpoint requested by the user.
